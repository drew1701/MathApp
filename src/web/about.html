About us

Kids Are Us is dedicated to making learning tools for kids fun and interactive. During these hard times, we wanted to build a Math App for 3rd graders and above. We understand that there are many learning games in the market however, they are not Free! So we thank you for your support and we hope your kids enjoy playing our simple and totally free app.

Mission

To support the community by providing a free learning materials.

Goal

To motivate kids to learn more in a simple and fun way

Your support is valuable , Together we will make a change !!!
